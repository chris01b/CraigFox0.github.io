Why You Should Join

All scouts are taught how to be a strong leader

We explore the great outdoors on monthly camping trips and other events

All of the scouts form a strong bond with each other and develop great friendships

The scouts learn life long skills such as first aid, swimming, camping, how to be a citizen, and much more

Our troop participates in numerous service trips including Eagle projects, highway clean ups, and campsite improvement

Our Troop Motto is "With Great Power Comes Great Responsibility"
