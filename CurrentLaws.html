Voting Act:

Any form of delaying a vote is illegal;
The Parliment must have at least 5 people for voting;
A simple majority is required for a vote to pass.

Narnian Annexation Act:

Narnia is annexed.

VAT Act:

VAT is the official acronym for Value Added Tax;
There is a 20% VAT.

National Currency Act:

Kreg is the official national currency;
One Kreg is a potato carved with a horizontal line through a K.

Elections Act:

Every 4 years, a secretary is elected for each department;
Every representative must vote for a candidate running during a position election;
All elections are voted through a majoritarian elimination system.

Secretary of the House Act:

The Secretary of the House manages current proposals and records votes.

Departments Act:

The Department of State tracks and manages foreign affairs;
The Department of Economics tracks economics and financing data;
The Department of Justice determines if any lawmakers violate any laws and/or ethical conduct;
The Department of the Interior tracks internal affairs;
The Department of Logistics tracks and manages communications and statistics.

Workers' Army Act:

600,000 citizens become members of the Workers' Army;
All 18 year old must sign up for the workers' army;
If more than the needed number of workers is acquired, the oldest workers are discharged;
At the age of 45, a worker is discharged;
If a doctor deems someone critically ill, they can not sign up and if already part of the workers' army, are immediately discharged;
The workers' army must pledge allegiance to the Cabinet;
The Parliment provides instruction for the workers' army.

Department Reform Act:

All departments have a secretary;
The Departments of State and Logistics have a undersecretary;
Secretaries and undersecretaries can only be representatives;
Each representative can hold 2 secretary positions and infinite undersecreatary positions;
During a time of war, the Undersecretary of State becomes the Secretary of War.
