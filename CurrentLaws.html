Voting Act:

Any form of delaying a vote is illegal;
The Parliment must have at least 5 people for voting;
A simple majority is required for a vote to pass;
Everyone must vote for a candidate running during a position election.

Narnian Annexation Act:

Narnia is annexed.

VAT Act:

VAT is the official acronym for Value Added Tax;
There is a 20% VAT.

National Currency Act:

Kreg is the official national currency;
One Kreg is a potato carved with a horizontal line through a K.

Elections Act:

Every 4 years, a secretary is elected for each department;
All elections are voted through a majoritarian system.

Secretary of the House Act:

The Secretary of the House manages current proposals and records votes.

Departments Act:

The Department of State tracks and manages foreign affairs;
The Department of Economics tracks economics and financing data;
The Department of Justice determines if any lawmakers violate any laws and/or ethical conduct;
The Department of the Interior tracks internal affairs;
The Department of Logistics tracks and manages communications and statistics.

Department Reform Act

All departments have a secretary;
The Departments of State and Logistics have a undersecreatary;
During a time of war, the Undersecreatary of State becomes the Secretary of War.
